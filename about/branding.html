Branding
I am a huge fan of the types of merchandise and graphic design that integrates parts of character designs into the visuals. Things such as Nyahallo's Genshin Bags and Yeurei's Genshin Cardigans are so cool to me -- being able to tell a character design by their visual motifs alone? sign me up! So here's a guide (for myself, and maybe someone else) to the sort of visual motifs I tend to associate with myself.
