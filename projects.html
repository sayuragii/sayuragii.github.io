(2022) Library of Ruina Avatars
Back in 2022 (around September), due to a lack of VRChat resources and avatars in the PM community, so I made a bunch of avatars of at least half of the sephirot to be used freely by people. I lost motivation by the time I was getting to Chesed. However, none of them have been removed.

In case you're wanting to try them out for yourself, I have a sheet of the avatar IDs to be used in Prismic's Avatar Search! I also have, if you're interested, all of the avatar as VRM files for free on my Ko-fi Shop.
